My name is Thomas McQuillan. I am a Photographer, Artist and Software Developer living in Sweden with my wife and two children. I am from Wellington, New Zealand where I graduated with a Bachelor of Design (honors) and a Master of Fine Arts (distinction) from Massey University's College of Creative Arts.

My Masters thesis titled 'The Sum of Things', explores the insidious creep of corporate consumerist culture within modern society, employing documentary photography as a critical tool of exploration and reflection.
